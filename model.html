Additional Support

Need Help Funding Your AP Program?

Establishing an AP African American Studies program at your school often requires institutional funding — and navigating the grant landscape can be overwhelming. As part of the Learn the Model program, we offer dedicated support to help educators research, identify, and apply for grants that can fund curriculum development, teacher training, and program launch.

This includes federal education grants, state-level humanities funding, private foundation support, and College Board program grants — many of which specifically support AP course expansion in underserved and first-adopter schools.
