Our Purpose

Most of us were handed a story about America — and we believed it, because we had no reason not to. But parts of that story were incomplete. Not because of who you are. Because of what was left out of what you were given. This is what was left out. Sourced. Documented. And told by someone you can trust.

Your Guide

Mr. Anthony Green

Teacher · Coach · Bishop O'Dowd High School, Oakland CA

Since 1986, Mr. Green has taught history at Bishop O'Dowd — one of Oakland's most distinguished college-preparatory institutions. He developed and leads their Advanced Placement African American Studies curriculum, and his approach is anything but passive. Field trips to Lois The Pie Queen, the Black Panthers museum, Marcus Bookstore — the oldest Black bookstore in America. He puts students inside history, not just in front of it.

His students don't just read about redlining — they map it across Oakland's neighborhoods with a UC Berkeley geographer as their critic. They don't just hear about the Great Migration — they trace it to the block they live on. For nearly four decades, Mr. Green has done something quietly radical: told the full story, sourced and documented, to anyone willing to listen.

He is not an activist. He is a teacher. And that distinction — the calm authority of a person who has spent 37 years getting the facts right — is exactly why this platform exists.
