Real Black People You Don't
See.

Media shapes perception. And for decades, the dominant media narrative around Black people has been a narrow, distorted slice of reality — crime, poverty, conflict. Meet real Black people that don't make the front page.

"When you only ever see one image of a people, you start to believe that image is the whole truth. It never is." — Mr. Anthony Green, Bishop O'Dowd High School
